Elite Trekker

Welcome To Elite Trekker A New Tool To Run in Your Browser to help you in your efforts to try and track your current exploration trip, Please keep in mind Credits earned are approximate so both Min & Max values are given.

How To Use

To Use The Tool is Simple , In the 1st Box (Discovered) Type In How many Objects of that type are already Discovered (By Another CMDR), The 2nd Box is how many of the object you are "claiming" , the tool adds the two fields up so a 1 in discovered and a 1 in 1st discoveries means that you've scanned two objects of that type one is discovered and one is "claimed" by You.
